## ğŸ“ˆ Scaling Considerations

### **Current Architecture (Free Tier)**
- **Frontend**: CDN-distributed, scales automatically
- **Backend**: Single shared VM on Fly.io
- **Database**: Neon serverless (auto-scales compute)
- **Storage**: R2 (globally distributed)

### **When to Scale**
- **> 100 concurrent users**: Upgrade Fly.io to dedicated VM
- **> 10 GB data**: Upgrade Neon plan
- **> 10 GB files**: Monitor R2 usage (pay-per-use)

### **Cost at Scale**
- 1,000 MAU: ~$5/month (Firebase Auth: $5)
- 100 GB database: ~$50/month (Neon Pro: $19 + storage)
- 100 GB files: ~$2/month (R2: $1.50/100GB)
- **Total**: ~$57/month for 1,000 users
